### [1] [Image-based investigation of the zebrafish developmental process using in vivo dynamic and multi-contrast optical coherence tomography](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.12655)

The uncertainties of $n^*_{\text{GaSb}}$ and $n_{\text{AlAsSb}}$ are quantified using a Monte Carlo-based approach. Our results provide accurate complex refractive index values for GaSb, which are vital to advance photonics-related technologies in the near- and mid infrared spectral region.
