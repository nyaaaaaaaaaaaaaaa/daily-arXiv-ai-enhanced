Method: 使用HAL QCD合作组生成的(2+1)-味格点配置，在物理点（π介子质量约137 MeV，格点间距约0.084 fm）进行计算。应用HAL QCD方法分析D-N系统的四点关联函数，获得相互作用核的导数展开的领头阶势，进而提取低能D-N散射的s波相移。

Result: I=0和I=1通道都有短程排斥核和中长程浅吸引势，但I=0通道比I=1通道更具吸引力。D-N势比其奇异区类似物KN势更具吸引力。I=0通道在低能区显示弱吸引行为，散射长度为0.246±0.105 fm；I=1通道显示排斥，散射长度为-0.086±0.050 fm。两个同位旋通道均未发现束缚态。

Conclusion: D-N系统在s波中不存在束缚态，表明该系统中不存在五夸克态。研究提供了D-介子与核子相互作用的首个格点QCD结果，为理解重味强子与核子相互作用提供了重要基准。

Abstract: We report the first lattice QCD study of the $s$-wave scattering of the $\bar{D}$-meson and the nucleon at the physical point, utilizing (2+1)-flavor configurations generated by the HAL QCD collaboration with a pion mass of $m_π\simeq 137$ MeV and a lattice spacing of $a\simeq0.084$ fm. By applying the HAL QCD method to the four-point correlation function of the $\bar{D}N$ system, we obtain a leading-order potential of the derivative expansion of the interaction kernel, which is then used to extract the $s$-wave phase shifts of low-energy $\bar{D}N$ scattering. Both the isospin $I=0$ and $I=1$ channels have a short-range repulsive core and a shallow attractive pocket in the intermediate to long-range region, though the $I=0$ channel is more attractive than the $I=1$ channel. We also observe that the $\bar{D}N$ potential exhibits more attraction than the $KN$ potential, which is its analog in the strange sector. In terms of the $s$-wave phase shifts, the $I=0$ channel shows a weak attractive behavior in the low-energy region with a positive scattering length of $0.246 \pm 0.105 (_{-0.051}^{+0.084})$ fm, whereas the $I=1$ channel shows repulsion with a negative scattering length of $-0.086 \pm 0.050 (_{-0.001}^{+0.037})$ fm. No bound states are found in both isospin channels, indicating the absence of a pentaquark state in the $s$-wave $\bar{D}N$ system.
